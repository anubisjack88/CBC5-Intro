Baltasar The Great

Player:Gemar Johnston

Baltasar is a fictional character from a drunken night of Dungeons and Dragons. Baltasar is a man who enjoys the open sea and having a flagon of ale with evey meal. When he is not drinking he is exsploring with his compainons whom he met at a dive bar of sorts. This man is a stout fellow who stands at about 5'11 and has an athletic build. Strong will and determination fuel his goal of one day obtaining his own ship. Our hero also enjoys his companion who play music that he often drinks too and passes out with a grunt and a smile.

Baltasar started his life as a child who had a good life. He was raised his parents who made him attend school during his early years and his teachers always told him he would be something great in life. Baltasar lived on the docks with his family and this was where he learned about the life of a sailor. He would watch the men come into port, make fast the lines, and prep for the next voyages at sea. He always had a burning desire to travle as it was in his blood. He came from a long line of men who took to the sea in search of glory, honor, war, and plunder. Many of the veteran sailors took a liking to him as he would bring them bread and ale. They would also teach him to use a sowrd much to his mothers disaproval.

When Baltasar was six winters old, he was made orphan. Little is known about this event but it changed the course of of his life forever. Baltasar became homeless and lived on scraps. The men who used to help him and train him had been called out to sea. This left him with people who would often try to abuse him. He soon found refuge in the slums of the docks. During this time, Baltasar was attacked by a varg who took him for a basic lad. Baltasar had a knife that he plunged into the attacker who exspired soon after. He learned that he needed to fight to get out of his nightmare.
